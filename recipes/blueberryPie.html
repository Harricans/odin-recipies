Blueberry Pie Recipe

The ultimate Blueberry Pie Recipe! It's simple to make, bursting with sweet,
juicy berries and topped with a classic lattice crust.
All you need is a generous scoop of vanilla ice cream over your warm slice of pie.

Ingredients 🛒

  • Pie crust
  • Blueberries
  • Lemon
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Egg

Method 🧑‍🍳

  1. Make the crust
  2. Prepare the lattice strips
  3. Preheat the oven
  4. Make the filling
  5. Weave the lattice crust
  6. Make the egg wash
  7. Bake
